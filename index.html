Til diya Murgi Mangxo (Chicken with Black Sesame Seeds)

Til diya Murgi. Country chicken. A generous slather of black sesame paste. The rage of peppercorn. The warmth of ginger. This is comfort food at its sublime best !!

Ingredients

  • 500 g chicken curry-cut pieces
  • 4 tbsp black sesame seeds lightly toasted and soaked in warm water for 10 minutes
  • 8-10 black peppercorn
  • 4 big onion finely chopped
  • 2 tsp ginger finely chopped
  • 3-4 cloves garlic finely chopped
  • 8-9 green chili slit
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • 3 tbsp mustard oil
  • salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Make a smooth paste of black sesame seeds and black peppercorn with very little water. Keep aside.
  2. Heat oil in a pan.
  3. When smoking hot, throw in the ginger, garlic and onions, saute till the onion is a gorgeous golden brown.
  4. Now add the chicken, turmeric powder, cumin powder and green chilies, cook over a medium flame for 5-7 odd minutes.
  5. Add Â½ cup warm water, continue to cook over a medium flame till the chicken is about to 75% cooked, 10-12 mins I would say.
  6. Add the black sesame and peppercorn paste, give it all a hearty mix.
    Splash a little water if it is becoming a bit too dry.
  7. Cook till the chicken is tender, another 5 minutes or so.
  8. Serve hot with rice.
